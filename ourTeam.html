Operation and Logistics

Operation and Logistic department handles the acquisition and management of logistical items such as venue,food and event protocol. Also in charge of securing speakers, security and flow of event. As such, they help to ensure the participants get the best experience possible.

Finance and Sponsorship department drafts budget and sponsorship packages. They determine budget for the conference and oversees cash flow for the event. Also, they work hard to secure sponsors, maintain good relationship with donors and ensure sponsors needs are met.

Human Resources

Human Resources department is in charge of the administrative process of the team, including recording meetings and department progress. This department will work to ensure all formal communication with participants and sponsors well-documented, while handling main queries regarding the event and be the point of contact for the coordinators.
