About the event

The Malaysian Students’ Technology Conference (MSTC) is the first initiative spearheaded by Malaysian students studying in the UK that serves as a platform for topics that are relevant to STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) students.

Under the banner of Young Malaysian Engineers (YME) the conference will cover a wide range of topics within the scope of entrepreneurship, social, environment and corporate. Not only will the participants get to have better insights on the local challenges faced in Malaysia, they will also have the opportunity to network with people from the industry.

About YME

Young Malaysian Engineers (YME) is a United Kingdom-based student organization built to empower Malaysian youth in the fields of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Maths (STEM). Founded in 2016, YME is one of the fastest-growing, STEM-oriented student bodies with 200+ registered members and a combined 2000+ followers on all social media platforms.

Dr. Jasmine Hau Yuan Wen

Topic: Keynote

Dr. Jasmine Hau Yuan Wen is a research fellow and the head of Cardiac Informatics Cluster at IJN-UTM Cardiovascular Engineering Centre, Universiti Teknologi Malaysia. She is also a senior lecturer at the Faculty of Biosciences and Medical Engineering, Universiti Teknologi Malaysia. She received both Ph.D. degree and master degree in electrical engineering from Universiti Teknologi Malaysia in 2009 and 2005, respectively. Dr. Hau's research interests include various fields such as processors, data security application, biomedical applications and many others.

• Over 50 published papers

• Owns a nationa l patent and copyrights

• Member of IEEE

Pedro Uria Recio
